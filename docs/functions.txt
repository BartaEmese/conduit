home screen: presenting the main menu, and the general content for anonymous or logged in users
registration: allowing anonymous users who don't have yet accoutns to register themselves
login: allow registered users to log in in order to get more functionality
new article: allow logged in users to create new articles
new comment: allow logged in users to comment on existing articles
(un)favorite articles: allow logged in users to favorite or unfavorite existing articles
(un)follow authores: allow logged in users to follow and unfollow other authors
my feed: show logged in users a feed of articles written by followed authors
my articles: show logged in users a feed of articles they wrote
artiles details: allow users to view details of articles
edit/delete my article: allow logged in users to browse and edit or even delete their articles
